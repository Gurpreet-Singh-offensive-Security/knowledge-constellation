## вңҰ The 60-second pitch

> You finish a course. You *get* the logic. Then you open a real system вҖ” a real request, a real chart, a real workflow вҖ” and there are twenty things you've never seen, and your brain just goes blank.

This fixes that. Instead of reading a concept top-to-bottom like a textbook, you **see** it: the core idea glowing in the middle, the major pieces branching out, every tiny detail hanging off as its own little star. Your eyes do the remembering.

Then you do it again for the next topic. And the next. Each one becomes its own **galaxy**, floating in your personal universe of knowledge.

Works for *anything* вҖ” IT, security, healthcare, finance, biology, law, whatever you're stuck on.

```

```

**1 В· Grab a free Gemini key** вҶ’ https://aistudio.google.com/apikey

**2 В· Open the app** вҶ’ just double-click `index.html`. That's the entire install.

**3 В· Paste the key, type a topic, hit Map.** Your first galaxy is born.

> Free tier has a per-minute request limit. If it asks you to slow down, wait ~30s. Your key is remembered, so you only paste it once.

## вңҰ If you see an error, don't panic

<details>
<summary><b>[ ! ]  The "what's really going on" cheat sheet</b></summary>

<br>

Most "errors" aren't your fault *or* the app's вҖ” they're just Google's free servers having a busy moment. The app auto-retries before it ever bothers you, so usually you won't even notice.

| What it says | What's really going on | What to do |
|---|---|---|
| **"Servers overloaded" (503)** | Google's side is momentarily slammed. Normal, temporary. | App auto-retries. If it persists, wait a few seconds & try again, or switch model. |
| **"Sending requests too fast" (429)** | Free tier allows only a handful per minute, and you went over. | Wait ~30s. For heavy expanding, switch to **gemini-2.5-flash-lite**. |
| **"API key was rejected"** | Key mistyped or copied wrong. | Re-copy it from [Google AI Studio](https://aistudio.google.com/apikey). |
| **"Model isn't available"** | That model isn't on your key. | Pick **gemini-2.5-flash** in the dropdown. |
| **"Couldn't reach Google"** | Your internet blinked. | Check your connection and retry. |

> Mental model: the free API is like calling a popular restaurant. Sometimes the line's just busy вҖ” call back in a few seconds and you're in. Nothing is broken.

**Going for a giant universe?** You can add galaxies and expand stars almost endlessly. The only real speed limit is how fast *one free key* may call Google вҖ” pause a couple seconds between requests (or use the lite model) and you'll sail past the busy-server hiccups.

</details>
